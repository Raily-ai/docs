## Requirements

To connect Raily to Claude, you need:

- **Claude Pro or Team subscription** - Custom MCP connectors are only available on paid Claude plans
- **Raily account** with configured data

## Setup Steps

Follow these steps to connect Raily to Claude:

### Step 1: Open Claude Settings

Click on your profile or settings icon in Claude.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/1 claude main.png" alt="Claude main interface" />
</Frame>

### Step 2: Navigate to Settings

Open the Settings menu.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/2 claude settings.png" alt="Claude settings menu" />
</Frame>

### Step 3: Go to Connectors

Select the Connectors section in settings.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/3 claude connectos.png" alt="Claude connectors section" />
</Frame>

### Step 4: Add Raily Connector

Click to add a new connector and enter your Raily MCP connection details.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/4 claude add connector.png" alt="Add Raily connector" style={{maxWidth: '450px', margin: '0 auto', display: 'block'}} />
</Frame>

### Step 5: Connector Added

Your Raily connector is now active and Claude can access your data.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/5 claude connector added.png" alt="Raily connector added successfully" />
</Frame>
